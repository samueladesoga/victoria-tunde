About


Victoria Tunde is an independent gospel singer and songwriter who was born and raised in London.

As a long serving choir member and lead singer at her Church, this seasoned banker with over 18 years in the financial services industry has juggled a busy work life and her commitment as a passionate worship leader.

Her love and devotion for God have continued to propel her incredibly expressive voice in ways which have ministered God’s peace, His love and purity. Her rich blend of musical tones tell stories with a propensity to capture her audience as well as advance an atmosphere for worship and a vehicle into God’s presence.

In December 2018, Victoria stormed the gospel music scene with her debut single “Changed Me” - a fulfilment of a God given assignment (2 Tim 1:7). Her sound has been described as new and is birthed out of her personal walk with God. Married for 12 solid years to the love of her life, Oluwaseyi Babatunde - a wonderful, supportive and spirit led man. Together they are raising two blessed, beautiful and bright children (Grace and Ruth).

Growing up in inner city London, Victoria developed a passion for mentoring and coaching young people. Over the years she has helped many young people reach their full professional and entrepreneurial potentials. Her catchphrase is to ‘always leave people better than when you met them’ and as a result has carried with her the maxim, “pay it forward”.

This is only the beginning so watch this space!
